## 8-Player Single-Elimination Tournament Brackets

The server matches 8 players of similar rating ranges into a Blitz Tournament. Upon matchmaking completion, the system generates a single-elimination tournament tree containing:
- **4 Quarterfinals Nodes** (`Q1` to `Q4`)
- **2 Semifinals Nodes** (`S1`, `S2`)
- **1 Finals Node** (`F1`)

### Bracket Advancement Synchronization
1. Each match runs in parallel on a 60-second timer.
2. Players completing their match early are transitioned to the **Tournament Lobby View**, showing progress bars of other parallel matches.
3. Once a round concludes, the engine provisions a new `RoomID` and sends a 5-second countdown to the advancing candidates.

---

## Live Spectating Hub & Reactions

Eliminated players or dashboard navigators can tap any active node in the tournament to spectate.
- **Dual-Stream Tracking**: Spectators receive a side-by-side feed detailing both competitors' active questions, current scores, and ghost typing input streams.
- **Floating Emoji Expressions**: Spectators can broadcast floating emojis (рҹ”Ҙ, рҹӨҜ, вҡЎ). These triggers send coordinates to the room socket, rendering floating canvas animations on all viewers' screens.
