## Game Modes

Elo offers three distinct competitive game modes to test your mental math capacity:

### 1. Ranked 1v1 Duels
Match against players of similar skill ranges globally.
- Each duel runs for **60 seconds**.
- An adaptive bot registers if matchmaking takes longer than **20 seconds**.
- Correct responses earn rating points, climbing from **Bronze** to **Grandmaster**.

### 2. 8-Player Blitz Tournaments
Compete in structured, single-elimination knockout trees.
- Advancing candidates receive new round keys in the **Tournament Lobby**.
- Parallel games run in real-time, displaying live progression markers.

### 3. Private Friendly Duels
Host or join room sessions using custom duration constraints, operators toggles (e.g. enabling multiplication or division), and shared room codes.

## Progression & Customizations

### Daily Streaks & Levels
Solving questions consistently builds your streak multiplier, increasing credit boosts and unlocking exclusive player titles.

### Cosmetics Vault
Exchange earned credits inside the dashboard to unlock premium UI themes (e.g. Neon Ghost, Cyberpunk, Light Mode) altering the client's canvas skins.

## Right to Be Forgotten & Privacy Settings

Elo respects player data autonomy. Under CCPA/GDPR rules, you can delete your profile:
1. Navigate to the **Settings & Privacy** tab on the Home dashboard.
2. Scroll to the **Right to Be Forgotten** section.
3. Tap **Delete Account**. This schedules permanent erasure after a **7-day grace period** (which you can cancel at any time).
